Relaxing Ipsum (Bob Ross)

Feb 5th, 2017

We'll throw some old gray clouds in here just sneaking around and having fun. If these lines aren't straight, your water's going to run right out of your painting and get your floor wet. We'll put a happy little sky in here.

You've got to learn to fight the temptation to resist these things. Just let them happen. I get carried away with this brush cleaning. You don't have to spend all your time thinking about what you're doing, you just let it happen.

For the lack of a better word I call them hangy downs. This is where you take out all your hostilities and frustrations. It's better than kicking the puppy dog around and all that so. Volunteering your time; it pays you and your whole community fantastic dividends. How to paint. That's easy. What to paint. That's much harder. I started painting as a hobby when I was little. I didn't know I had any talent. I believe talent is just a pursued interest. Anybody can do what I do. Let's build an almighty mountain.

Everything is happy if you choose to make it that way. Don't kill all your dark areas - you need them to show the light. A tree cannot be straight if it has a crooked trunk. You don't have to be crazy to do this but it does help.
